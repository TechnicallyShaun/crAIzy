## Design Principles

### 1. **Human in the Loop**
AI proposes, human disposes. Every significant action requires human approval. You stay in control.

### 2. **Transparency**
See exactly what agents are doing, thinking, and planning. No black boxes.

### 3. **Composability**
Small, focused agents that do one thing well. Combine them for complex workflows.

### 4. **Terminal Native**
Built for developers who live in the terminal. Fast, keyboard-driven, no browser required.

### 5. **Provider Agnostic**
Works with any AI provider. Swap models without changing workflows.
